**Assignment title:** Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

**Prompt** 
Create a poster of a science fiction blockbuster that features the landscape of a futuristic city that displays robots enjoying the city and humans enclosed in apartments working on computers. The city resembles New York City, and the buildings should be enlarged, creating thin and large vertical lines that, at the top are transformed into organic lines showing the deformation of society at the top. The Empire State Building should be the largest shape, elongated and distorted into a more organic shape. The focus of the composition is on the robots that are walking and having fun in the city. This should contrast with the small shapes of humans that are inside their apartments. We can look at them through the windows. The texture of the city feels very metallic and rough as a way to communicate that the robots dominate the scene. The mass of the robots should feel heavy. There should be contrast between the colors outdoors, which should be dark and blue, and the brighter and warmer colors of the humans inside the apartments as a way to communicate the love that only humans can bring to social life.

**Vocabulary Breakdown**
Lines: The basic element that defines shapes and provides direction to the viewers.
Shape: An object or a form. It can be geometric or organic.
Texture: The physical sensation of an object.
Volume: The space that something occupies. 
Contrast: Two different states of the same elements. Two different forms of lines, shapes, or colors.
