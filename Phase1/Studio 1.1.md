**Assignment title:** Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

**Prompt** 
Create a large-scale mural painted in acrylic on an urban building wall, designed as a form of social protest against climate change. The artwork should be located in a busy city square where it can inspire public discussion. Its main message is to raise awareness about environmental destruction and the urgency of sustainable living. The subject matter includes melting glaciers, endangered animals, and industrial smokestacks. The style should be representational with bold colors and dynamic composition to emphasize contrast and movement, symbolizing the tension between nature and industry.

**Vocabulary Breakdown**
Medium: The materials, tools, and processes used to make the artwork (it can be a mural, painting, drawing, etc).
Meaning: The main message
Subject Matter: The "what" in the piece, what we can see. 
Representational Art: The style in which we can recognize the figures and shapes in the composition.
